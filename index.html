Welcome to the Balloon Game

How to Play:

  1. Press the "Pump Balloon" button to inflate the balloon and earn points.
  2. The bigger the balloon, the more points you can get.
  3. But be careful! If the balloon pops, you lose the points for that round.
  4. Press the "Collect Points" button before the balloon pops to save your points.
  5. The game consists of 20 rounds. Collect as many points as you can!
